Libreya – Classic Literature, Reimagined

Libreya is a free digital library dedicated to classic literature. The platform offers more than 300 timeless masterpieces from some of the world's greatest authors, carefully formatted for a modern reading experience. Readers can explore novels, philosophical works, and influential texts that have shaped literature and culture throughout history.

Discover the World's Greatest Books

From the novels of Jane Austen and Charles Dickens to the philosophical writings of Fyodor Dostoevsky and Leo Tolstoy, classic literature remains one of the most important foundations of global culture and storytelling. Libreya brings these works together into a single, beautifully designed reading platform.

Each book is presented in a clean and readable format, making it easy to explore historic works of fiction, drama, and philosophy on any device. Readers can browse by author, discover influential titles, and immerse themselves in the ideas and narratives that have inspired generations.

A Free Library for Everyone

Libreya is built around a simple idea: classic literature should be accessible to everyone. All books available through the platform are part of the public domain and can be read completely free. There are no subscriptions, paywalls, or limitations — just great books made available for modern readers.

Read Timeless Stories

Whether you are discovering classic literature for the first time or revisiting beloved works, Libreya makes it easy to explore hundreds of enduring masterpieces. Open the app to begin reading some of the most important books ever written.
