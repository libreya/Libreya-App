Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen

First published in 1813, Pride and Prejudice is one of the most beloved classic novels in English literature. It explores themes of love, reputation, and social standing in early 19th century England.

Libreya presents the full text in a clean, readable online format for free. Students, teachers, and literature lovers can explore the story anywhere, on any device.

About the Author

Jane Austen (1775–1817) is one of the most celebrated English novelists, known for her sharp social commentary and enduring love stories.
