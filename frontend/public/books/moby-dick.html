Moby Dick – Herman Melville

First published in 1851, Moby Dick tells the story of Captain Ahab's quest to hunt the great white whale. It is a tale of adventure, obsession, and the human struggle against nature.

About the Author

Herman Melville (1819–1891) was an American novelist, short story writer, and poet. He is best known for Moby Dick, a masterpiece of American literature.
